ABOUT US

What's dessert without a little love and a lot of goodness?

Fill Le Heart was founded in 2022, by 2 brothers, with a kitchen next to their house in Kerala, Thrissur & a big dream to ensure that people across India get access to the most famous traditional dessert of Oman, Omani Halwa and many more unique food experience.

VISION, MISSION & VALUES

Back in 2022, we were selling our products with the help of social media to limited areas in Kerala. Today we are able to serve across India with trusted and best delivery partners. It's the love and support of thousands of people that has brought us this far. People who share our love for authentic, traditional dessert.

Vision

Our vision at Fill Le Heart is to be the leading and trusted provider of unique, fresh and preservative-free sweets and ready-to-eat foods for inspiring people to embrace a healthier, happier and sustainable lifestyle. Through our relentless pursuit of quality, innovation and sustainable practices, we aim to be the trusted choice for everyone seeking authentic Omani halwa and time-saving food solutions.

Mission

At Fill Le Heart, our mission is to offer authentic, fresh and preservative-free food that doesn't just fulfil your cravings for traditional taste, but also fulfils your heart.

Values

At Fill Le Heart, we are committed to preserving the rich legacy of Omani halwa by blending traditional recipes with premium ingredients, creating artisanal sweets that not only satisfy your cravings but also bring you closer to the warmth of culture, family, and cherished memories.
